Effect of upscaling

(Without image) Best Quality uses Spline or Mitchell
depending on scaling up or down
Stretch is not a filter, but a stretching of the pixels without interpolation
BoxEquivalent to Stretch with high quality and pixel-centered coordinates
Linear Linear interpolation results in a transition between pixels
Half Cosine Mixture of Linear and Cosine results in a medium transition between the pixels
Cosine Cosine-like interpolation results in a faster transition between pixels
Bicubic Simple bi-cubic filter (blurred)
Mitchell The Mitchell filter is a good interpolation for downscaling.
Spline Good filter for scaling up
Lanczos 2 Lanczos filter with radius 2 and blur correction
Lanczos 3 Lanczos filter with radius 3, high contrast
Lanczos 4 Lanczos filter with radius 4, high contrast
